Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CalcVerse 360+ ("the App"), developed by Rafhan Chaudhary under the developer account Skyloop apps, collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require any login or personal details. We do not collect your name, email, address, or contacts.

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

The following non-personal data may be collected automatically:

  • Device model & operating system
  • App activity and usage analytics
  • Country (approximate, not GPS)
  • Crash logs / performance diagnostics

1.2 Calculator Inputs

All values you enter into calculators (e.g., percentage, unit conversion, currency conversion, mathematical and financial tools) are processed locally on your device. We do not store or transmit this data.

2. Third-Party Services We Use

2.1 Google AdMob (Ads)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information and interaction data
  • Approximate location (non-GPS)
  • Usage patterns for ad relevance and fraud prevention

You may disable personalized ads using your device settings.

2.2 RevenueCat (Subscriptions)

The App uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history (e.g., active subscription status)
  • Your app user ID (non-personal)
  • Country/region (from store)

RevenueCat does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or payment card details. All payments are processed securely by Google Play or Apple App Store.

2.3 Currency Conversion API

For live exchange rates, the App sends only the currency pair (e.g., USD → PKR). No personal or device data is transmitted.

3. How We Use the Information

Non-personal data is used to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Enhance calculator features
  • Manage subscriptions and premium access
  • Display ads effectively

We never sell your data or share personal information.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your data. Since the App does not store personal information, the privacy risk is minimal.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect child data.

6. Your Choices

  • Disable personalized ads from device settings.
  • Uninstall the App to stop all data collection.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use means acceptance of the updated policy.
